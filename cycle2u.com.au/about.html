Paul Captein - Your Mechanic

Paul, the youngest of three brothers has been involved in competitive cycling since the age of seven. The eldest brother going on to win a silver medal in the commonwealth games as a track cyclist in 1982.

Paul has had successes in road,track and in his early thirties as a masters B.M.X. rider. This combined with over thirteen years as a professional bicycle mechanic, has helped develop an understanding of needs for cyclists of all levels, from the enthusiast to the serious competitor.

Knowing the importance of positioning for comfort and performance as well as requirements of ultimate mechanical workings. Paul is always happy to share his knowledge from experience and will help out wherever needed.
